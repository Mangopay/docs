### Description

The Payconiq PayIn object allows platforms to process payments made with their Payconiq mobile app, or via banking apps that support Payconiq. 

<Warning>
**Deprecated â€“ Payconiq being discontinued end of 2025**

The Payconiq payment method will no longer be supported after December 31, 2025, and the Payconiq endpoint will be decommissioned no longer supported.

Platforms using Payconiq are invited to integrate [Bancontact](/guides/payment-methods/bancontact), which allows users to continue paying with their Payconiq by Bancontact mobile app via the `DeepLinkURL`.

Following its acquisition by the European Payments Initiative (EPI), the Payconiq brand and services are being phased out. <a href="https://www.payconiq.be/en/payconiq-evolves" target="_blank">Read more</a> **&rarr;**
</Warning>
