Mangopay relies on the Direct Card PayIn to process payments with a registered card. This means that you need to first go through the card registration process to be able to process a direct card pay-in.  

<Note>  
**Best practice – Pay-in to the author’s wallet** 
  
Funds should be credited in two steps:  
Pay-in to the author’s wallet.  
Transfer to the credited user’s wallet.  
</Note>
