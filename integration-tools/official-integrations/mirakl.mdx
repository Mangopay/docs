## Official connector for Mirakl  

Modular payment infrastructure for your Mirakl marketplace  
- No hosting required : Go live fast and without impacting your roadmap with our hosted connector  
- Compliance : The Mangopay Connector for Mirakl is certified by Mirakl, and trusted by leading marketplaces across Europe  
- Flexibility : Built with flexibility in mind, it allows you to choose what payment scenario best fits your needs, granularly  
- Automation : From order processing to payouts, key tasks are automated

## Getting started  

<Steps> 
    <Step>Create a Mangopay account. Get started.</Step> 
    <Step>Determine which payment workflows match your business needs: Choose and integrate the payment methods you wish (optional) or work with your existing PSP.</Step> 
    <Step>Request the activation of the connector with your Account Manager.</Step> 
    <Step>Set up your Mirakl environment to work with the Connector: set up your custom fields and documents.</Step> 
    <Step>Request the activation of the KYC feature from Mirakl.</Step> 
    <Step>Test the synchronization and onboarding of your sellers.</Step> 
    <Step>Test the synchronization of your invoices each billing cycle.</Step> 
    <Step>Test the execution of payouts to your sellers and your marketplace account.</Step> 
    <Step>Subscribe to connector webhooks.</Step> 
    <Step>Go live.</Step>  
</Steps>
