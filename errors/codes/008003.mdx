---
title: '008003'  
---


                    | **Error message**     | Stolen Card                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The pay-in failed because the issuing bank declared the card as stolen. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">Card</div><div class="pill">Fraud policy</div><div class="pill">Pay-in</div>                         |
                  

#### What to do  

In such cases, if end users did not declare their card as stolen, they need to contact their bank to unblock the situation.
