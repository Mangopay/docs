#### Additional information  

Depending on the type of pay-in and your contract, there can be a maximum permitted amount for a transaction. For instance, the maximum pay-in amount by card is €2,500 by default.  

#### Causes  

The attempted pay-in amount is higher than the maximum amount.  

### What to do  

Either make sure that the transaction fits the contractual limits, or contact Mangopay’s team to adjust the platform’s thresholds if applicable.
