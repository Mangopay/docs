---
title: '002998'  
---


                    | **Error message**     | Blocked due to the Bank Account User's KYC limitations (maximum debited or credited amount reached)                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The payout has been refused refused because the bank account owner is not verified. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">Payout</div><div class="pill">User verification</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error occurs when the user who is defined as the Owner of the bank account is not verified. Payouts can only be made to verified users.  

#### What to do  

The end user needs to go through the verification process in order to be able to make a payout.
