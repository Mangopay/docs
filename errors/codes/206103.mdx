#### Causes

The bank is not supported on Mangopay's Pay by Bank solution. 

#### What to do

Check the list of [supported banks](/guides/payment-methods/banking/pay-by-bank#supported-banks). Retry the payment with one of the listed values or no value, allowing the user to select in session.
