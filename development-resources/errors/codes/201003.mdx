---  
 title: '201003'  
---  


                    | **Error message**     | PayPal transaction approval has expired                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The pay-in failed because the PayPal account owner did not confirm the payment. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">PayPal</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error is triggered when the PayPal account owner did not confirm the payment within 3 hours and thus, the session expired.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, the end user needs to make a new payment and confirm it within 3 hours.
