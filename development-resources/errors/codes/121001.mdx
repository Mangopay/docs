 title: '121001'  
---  


                    | **Error message**     | The bank wire has been refused                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The bank wire has been refused by Mangopay or canceled by the end user. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">Payout</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error is triggered when the bank wire has been refused by Mangopay or canceled by the end user.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, platforms can:  
-Contact the end user to know more about the reasons for canceling the bank wire.  
-Contact Mangopay to know more about the reasons for canceling the bank wire.
