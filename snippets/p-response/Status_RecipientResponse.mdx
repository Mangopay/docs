<ResponseField name="Status" type="string">
**Possible values:** `PENDING`, `CANCELLED`, `ACTIVE`, `DEACTIVATED`

The status of the recipient: 
- `PENDING` – The user must complete SCA before the recipient can become `ACTIVE`. 
- `CANCELLED` – SCA was not successful and the recipient creation request was cancelled. To retry, create another recipient to retrieve another `PendingUserAction.RedirectUrl`.
- `ACTIVE` – SCA was successful and the recipient is ready to be used for payouts.
- `DEACTIVATED` – The recipient has been permanently deactivated and can no longer be used.
</ResponseField>
