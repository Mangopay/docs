<ResponseField name="Status" type="string" >    
The status of the overall IDV Session:
- `PENDING` – The session is available at the `HostedUrl` and user action is required to complete it. 
- `REVIEW` – The session is under manual review by Mangopay. 
- `VALIDATED` – The session was successful.
- `REFUSED` – The session was refused.
- `OUTDATED` – The session was outdated.
</ResponseField>
