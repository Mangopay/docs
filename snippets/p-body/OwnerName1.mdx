<ParamField body="OwnerName" type="string" required>  
<MaxLength255Characters />     
The owner of the banking alias. The `OwnerName` must match the name of the user owning the wallet (`FirstName` and `LastName` for a <a href="/api-reference/users/natural-user-object">Natural User</a>, `Name` for a <a href="/api-reference/users/legal-user-object">Legal User</a>).

If Mangopay has provided your platform with a Technical Collection Virtual IBAN for reconciliation purposes, the `OwnerName` must be "Mangopay S.A." or "Mangopay S.A. - Your Trading Name". Please ensure your have confirmed this integration with our teams <a href="https://hub.mangopay.com/" target="_blank">via the Dashboard</a>.
