---  
 title: '001002'  
---  


                    | **Error message**     | Author is not the wallet owner                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The user Id used as Author has to be the wallet owner |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">Transfer</div><div class="pill">Wallet</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

The transfer `AuthorId` is different from the `Id` of the debited wallet `Owners`.  

#### What to do  

The author has to be the user being debited.
