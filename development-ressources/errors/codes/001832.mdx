---  
 title: '001832'  
---  


                    | **Error message**     | The payment has been cancelled                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The pay-in failed because the end user canceled it. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="pill">Mandate</div><div class="pill">Direct debit</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error occurs when end users contacted their bank to cancel the pay-in.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, platforms need to contact their customer to know more about the cancellation.

This error is returned on the Direct Direct-Debit PayIn object.
