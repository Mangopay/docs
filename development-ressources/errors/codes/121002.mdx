                    | **Error message**     | The author is not the wallet owner                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The payout author is not the corresponding wallet owner. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="label">Wallet</div><div class="label">Payout</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error is triggered when the payout `AuthorId` is different from the `UserId` of the Wallet from which the payout is made.
