**Note – Legacy error** 
  
You may not be able to reproduce the error.  
</Note>  

#### Causes  

This error may occur when the end user’s card doesn’t support the 3DS feature.  

#### What to do  

In such cases, end users can:  
-Contact their bank to ask for a card that supports the 3DSecure feature.  
-Choose another payment method.
