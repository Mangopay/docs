---  
 title: '008501'  
---  


                    | **Error message**     | Transaction refused due to blocked Country                   |
                    |-----------------------|--------------------------------------------|
                    | **Short description** | The payout cannot be performed because the target country is not authorized. |
                    | **Categories**        | <div class="label">Fraud policy</div><div class="label">Payout</div>                         |
                  

#### Causes  

This error is triggered when attempting to make a payout to a bank account domiciled in a country for which the payout feature is blocked.   

For more information, see the Restrictions by country article.
