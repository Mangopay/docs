### Statement descriptor available on BACS Direct Debit

The `StatementDescriptor` parameter can now be sent for <a href="/api-reference/direct-debit-payins/create-direct-debit-payin">direct debit pay-ins</a> on BACS mandates (as well as SEPA). 

For BACS, the length is truncated at 10 alphanumeric characters or spaces.

The `StatementDescriptor` is also available on <a href="/api-reference/refunds/create-refund-payin">pay-in refunds</a> of these two payment methods.

See the <a href="/bank-statements">Customizing bank statement references</a> article for details on this parameter.
