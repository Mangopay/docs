<span class="date">2021-09-08</span>  
  
  
<div class="pill pill-green">Added</div>  
  

More information are now available regarding refused KYC documents. Therefore, it will be easier for you to adapt your app behaviour and help your end users.  

Explanation are now available in a new parameter called `Flags` that has been added to the "View a KYC Document" endpoint. It will display one or several error codes indicating why your document validation has failed.   
<div class="pill pill-green">Added</div>  
  

We are now providing new hooks for our new Instant payouts feature:  
-INSTANT_PAYOUT_SUCCEEDED  
-INSTANT_PAYOUT_FALLBACKED  

It will allow you to trigger an action depends on the Instant Payout treatment.
